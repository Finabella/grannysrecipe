About Us

Granny's Pies has been crafting handmade pies from scratch for over 10 years. Our recipes have been passed down through our family, using only the finest local ingredients. Every pie is baked fresh daily in our family kitchen.

Whether you're craving a classic apple pie, a tangy lemon meringue, or a rich pecan pie, Granny's has something special waiting for you. Stop by our bakery or try one of our famous recipes at home!
